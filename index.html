ASTEROIDS

Ariel Hou, Sep 2015

How to Play

It's simple: you are the weird triangle/rectangle hybrid, and you want to shoot the circles ("asteroids")! Controls are:

  • up/down: move backward/forward
  • left/right: turn counter-clockwise/clockwise
  • spacebar: shoot (can hold down to run and gun)
  • z: hard brake (speed=0; the physics totally work)

Press spacebar to start!
